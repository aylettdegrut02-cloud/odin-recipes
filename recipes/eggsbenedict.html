Eggs Benedict

Home

Description

A decadent dish with English muffins, Candain-style bacon, and poached eggs topped off with rich hollandaise sauce.

Ingredients

Hollandaise Sauce

  • 4 egg yolks
  • 3 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice, or less to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of water
  • 1/8 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 pinch ground white pepper
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon hot water, or more as needed(optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, ot to taste

Eggs Benedict

  • 1 Teaspoon distilled white vinegar
  • 8 eggs
  • 8 strips canadian bacon
  • 4 english muffins, split
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened
